Problem Statement:
Given a Movie Gener Dataset (which has Summary of movies) and a search query, find the most relevent movies which match the search query.

Note: The Search queries are saved in .txt files, 2 types of search queries:
Single Word Search Query , Ex: Comedy
String Search Query, Ex:  Comedy with Action

Method:
Finding out the similarity between the search words and the summary of movies by TF-IDF method(Term Frequency and Inverse Document Frequency)
